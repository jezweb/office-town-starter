# The Office 💬

Home base of **@boss** — your primary contact. This is where you go to talk to the town: say what you want, and @boss routes it to the Workshop, Library, or Lookout and holds the thread.

@boss is a Goose agent; its brief ships in [`office-town-plugin`](https://github.com/jezweb/office-town-plugin) (`agents/boss.md`). This folder is its memory:

| Folder | What lives here |
|---|---|
| `journal/` | `YYYY-MM-DD.md` — the running log of what happened each day |
| `findings/` | patterns @boss noticed, for the Library to graduate into the wiki |
| `facts/` | facts @boss holds across sessions |
| `tasks/` | in-flight items |
| `inbox/` | things handed to @boss by you or the other roles |

The *work itself* lives in the wiki (`wiki/`), not here. This is the desk, not the filing cabinet.
