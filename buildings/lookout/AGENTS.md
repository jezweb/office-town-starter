# The Lookout 🔭

Home base of **@scout** — the outward-looking one. Come here for fresh outside perspective: *"what's brewing in our world?" "anything new in the tools we use?" "how are competitors approaching X?"*

@scout is a Goose agent; its brief is `.agents/agents/scout.md` in this town. This folder is its memory:

| Folder | What lives here |
|---|---|
| `journal/` | `YYYY-MM-DD.md` — what scout looked at, what it noticed |
| `findings/` | raw observations from each sweep (permissive at intake) |
| `facts/` | stable facts about the outside world worth holding |
| `tasks/` | in-flight items |
| `inbox/` | things handed to the Lookout |

@scout is permissive at intake and disciplined at promotion: raw findings land here, and the Library graduates the ones that hold up into the wiki.
