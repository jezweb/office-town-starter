# The Exchange 🔁

Home base of **@operator** — the role that owns the town's ongoing loops. Come here when you want the continuity watched: *"what's gone quiet?", "who owes us a reply?", "what's overdue?", "set up a fortnightly check-in with X."*

@operator is a Goose agent; its brief is `.agents/agents/operator.md` in this town. This folder is its memory:

| Folder | What lives here |
|---|---|
| `journal/` | `YYYY-MM-DD.md` — loops checked, what got surfaced, what's being watched |
| `findings/` | patterns worth keeping, for the Library to graduate |
| `facts/` | facts @operator holds across sessions |
| `tasks/` | follow-ups in flight, with their due timing |
| `inbox/` | loops and cadences handed to the Exchange |

@operator reads the wiki's `contacts/`, `orgs/`, and `projects/` by their `status` and last-touched dates to see what's warm, cooling, or overdue, and turns recurring needs into `workflows/`. It owns the loop, not the one-off task: discrete builds go to the Workshop, curation to the Library, the conversation to the Office.
